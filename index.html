Map of Locations

The Wilford Woodruff Papers Exploration provides insights into the locations referenced by Wilford Woodruff. It includes places he mentions in his writings, whether writing to, about, or from those locations. The Map of Locations feature displays these places visually, focusing on Woodruff's journal entries. By studying the journal entries within the context of specific locations, researchers can gain valuable insights into Woodruff's experiences and connections. Overall, the exploration offers a comprehensive exploration of Woodruff's geographic footprint and its significance in understanding his life and contributions.
